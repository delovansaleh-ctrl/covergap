FAQ

Questions, answered

Is this a sales pitch?+
No. The review is genuinely independent — we're not tied to any one insurer and we don't have quotas to meet. You'll get an honest picture of where you stand. If you're happy with your current cover, we'll tell you that. There's no pressure to change anything.
How much does this cost?+
Nothing. The review and adviser conversation are completely free. If you decide to make changes to your cover, the adviser is paid by the insurance provider, not by you.
What is life insurance?+
Life insurance provides financial protection for your family if you pass away. It can replace lost income, pay off debts, or cover expenses. Income protection is different — it replaces your income if you can't work due to illness or injury.
How much income protection do I need?+
It depends on your situation — typically between 60–80% of your income. If you have dependants or a mortgage, you might need more. An adviser will help you figure out what's right for you.
How long do life insurance policies take to pay out?+
Most life insurance claims are paid within 10–15 working days, though complex claims can take longer. Your adviser will explain the typical timeframe for your specific policy.
How do I get life insurance and what's the advantage of using an adviser?+
You can buy online, through your bank, or with an adviser. An independent adviser saves time by comparing options across multiple providers, ensures you get the right level of cover for your situation, and explains exactly what you're getting — no surprises.
